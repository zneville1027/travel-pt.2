What is Aruba?

Aruba is one of the lesser Antilles islands that is found off of the coast of Venuzuela. Its beaches are often viewed as a famous vacation spot due to its beauty.

 

Why You Should Visit Aruba

Aruba is a fantastic vacation spot for families and singles alike looking for an idealistic getaway. Out of all the Carribean islands, aruba was voted as having the least amount of rainfall. Maximizing the amount of time travelers have to experience the different activities that the Aruba has to offer involving a variety of outdoor sides, and indoor views. Don't let the sunny pictures fool you. Although the sun does shine, the trade winds keep the outdoors at a comfortably warm temperature of around 87 degrees. The culture and ambiance of Aruba is unmatched in the kindness of its native people and inhabitants that make any foreigners feel welcome! No matter who you are or where you come from, there is always a place for you in Aruba!
